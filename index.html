Capsul’in Zero Impact is the world first coffee capsule “OK Compost Home” certified, 100% Biobased, barrier to oxygen and compatible with the Nespresso system.

Capsul’in Zero Impact is solving many different problems linked to renewability of our resources, permeability of biomaterial and end of life options… all of this without losing quality in cup.

COMPLIANCE WITH THE FOLLOWING INTERNATIONAL STANDARDS

    Biobased

    Capsule made of a natural material derived from 100%
    Biobased cellulose and vegetal oils.

    Biobased refers to the feedstock, the beginning of the life cycle. A Biobased polymer is wholly or partly made from biomass which refer to organic material like trees, plants, crops, grasses… Biomass means renewable resource as opposed to fossil resource which are limited resources. The 4 stars OK biobased certification shows that Capsul’in IML Zero Impact, in compliance with the ASTM D6866 standard, has a biobased carbon content of 100%.












    Capsul’in IML Zero Impact is the unique and the first ever home compostable certified and 100% biobased coffee capsule ensuring a perfect result in cup thanks to a barrier to oxygen level which has never been achieved before and a totally innovative compostable top lid.

    A Biobased, Compostable, Oxygen barrier and non tearable filter top lid have also been developed along with Capsul’in Zero Impact to ensure perfect extraction and best result in cup.
